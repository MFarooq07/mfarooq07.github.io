About me

Hello! I recently graduated from Union College with honours in B.S. Computer Engineering, with a focus on Computer Science. My journey in this exciting field led me to my role as a Python programmer at Evolutionary Lab, Union College. For 3 years I delved into fascinating research on silicon robots, creating 2D simulations of real-world soft robots using MAP-Elites, and conducting experiments on the Chameleon Cloud comparing different algorithms in evolving the soft robots in simulation environments.

Outside of academics, I wear a couple of different hats. I was a captain, President and a Co-founder of Union College's Cricket Club, which has honed my leadership and teamwork skills. Additionally, as a Resident Advisor, I enjoyed building a supportive community and tackling challenges head-on.

This summer, I'm actively looking for jobs relevant to Computer Engineering (this may include jobs relevant to Electrical Engineering and Computer Science as well). This hands-on experience is a significant step towards my goal of pursuing a Master's or Ph.D. in soft robotics, robotics, and IoT in near future.
